WAYANAD

SPOT.
1

CHEMBRA PEAK

This is the highest peak in Wayanad and is a popular spot for trekking. It offers breathtaking views of the surrounding hills and valleys.

SPOT.
2

EDAKKAL CAVES

These ancient caves are known for their prehistoric rock carvings, which are believed to be over 6,000 years old. The caves also offer stunning panoramic views of the surrounding landscape.

SPOT.
3

BANASURA SAGAR DAM

This is the largest earth dam in India and is located amidst the Banasura Hills. The dam site also offers opportunities for boating and trekking, as well as picturesque views of the reservoir and surrounding mountains.

SPOT.
4

SOOCHIPARA WATERFALLS

Also known as Sentinel Rock Waterfalls, this three-tiered waterfall is a popular tourist attraction in Wayanad. Visitors can enjoy swimming in the pool at the base of the falls or go trekking through the surrounding forests.
