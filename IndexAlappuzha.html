ALLEPPEY BEACH

Alleppey Beach, also known as Alappuzha Beach, is a beautiful stretch of golden sand fringed by palm trees. It is a popular spot for relaxing and enjoying the serene beauty of the Arabian Sea. Visitors can enjoy activities such as beach volleyball, camel rides, and horse rides. The pier at the beach offers stunning views of the sunset.

SPOT.
2

KUMARAKOM BIRD SANCTUARY

The Kumarakom Bird Sanctuary is a haven for birdwatchers and nature enthusiasts. Located on the banks of Vembanad Lake, it is home to a variety of migratory birds such as Siberian cranes, herons, egrets, and teal. Visitors can explore the sanctuary by boat or on foot to observe the diverse avian species in their natural habitat.

SPOT.
3

MARARI BEACH

Marari Beach is a tranquil and pristine destination renowned for its idyllic setting and laid-back atmosphere. Set amidst coconut groves and lush vegetation, this beach offers opportunities for sunbathing, swimming in the clear waters, or simply relaxing under swaying palm trees. The area also features traditional fishing villages where visitors can experience local culture and cuisine.

SPOT.
4

PATHIRAMANAL ISLAND

Pathiramanal Island is an enchanting island located in Vembanad Lake near Alappuzha. Known for its scenic beauty and rich biodiversity, it offers an ideal escape into nature with its lush greenery and abundant birdlife. Boat cruises to Pathiramanal provide an opportunity to enjoy peaceful surroundings while spotting numerous species of birds such as kingfishers, egrets, herons, and migratory ducks.
