BEYPORE

This beach is famous for being the location where Portuguese explorer Vasco da Gama landed in 1498, marking the beginning of European exploration of India. The beach offers beautiful views and a peaceful atmosphere.

SPOT.
2

KAPPAD BEACH

Known for its ancient port and shipbuilding heritage, Beypore is a fascinating place to visit. Visitors can explore the traditional shipbuilding yard and witness the craftsmanship of building large wooden vessels.

SPOT.
3

MANANCHIRA SQUARE

This centrally located park features a large man-made pond, musical fountain, landscaped gardens, and an art gallery. It's a great place to relax and enjoy some leisure time in the heart of Kozhikode city.

SPOT.
4

THUSHARAGIRI WATERFALLS

Located about 50 kilometers from Kozhikode, these picturesque waterfalls are surrounded by lush greenery and offer opportunities for trekking, rock climbing, and nature walks.
