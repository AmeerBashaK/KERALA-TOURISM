Silent Valley National Park

This is a beautiful national park known for its rich biodiversity and pristine natural beauty. It's a great place for nature lovers and wildlife enthusiasts.

Malampuzha Dam and Gardens

This is a popular tourist destination known for its scenic gardens, amusement park, boating facilities, and the iconic cable car ride that offers stunning views of the surrounding area.

Nelliampathy Hills

Nelliampathy is a picturesque hill station with lush greenery, tea estates, and breathtaking viewpoints offering panoramic vistas of the Western Ghats.

SPOT.
4

Palakkad Fort

This historic fort was built by Hyder Ali of Mysore in the 18th century and offers an interesting glimpse into the region's past. The fort also has a well-maintained garden that adds to its charm.
