MUNNAR

Known for its picturesque tea gardens, rolling hills, and pleasant weather, Munnar is a popular hill station in Idukki. Visitors can explore the tea plantations, visit the Eravikulam National Park to spot the endangered Nilgiri Tahr, and enjoy activities like trekking and birdwatching.

SPOT.
2

RAMAKKALMEDU

Ramakkalmedu is a scenic hill station known for its panoramic views of the surrounding Western Ghats. It's also famous for its large windmills and the Kuravan and Kurathi statue that depicts a local tribal couple.

SPOT.
3

ANAMUDI

As the highest peak inSouth India, Anamudi is a popular attraction for trekkers and nature enthusiasts. The peak is part of the Eravikulam National Park and offers stunning views of the Western Ghats.

SPOT.
4

KUMILY

Kumily is a gateway town to the Periyar Tiger Reserve and Thekkady, making it a popular destination for wildlife enthusiasts. Visitors can enjoy boat rides on the Periyar Lake, go on jungle safaris, and explore spice plantations in this area.
