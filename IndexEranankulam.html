FORT KOCHI

Known for its colonial architecture, diverse culture, and beautiful beaches, Fort Kochi is a historic area with attractions like St. Francis Church, Chinese Fishing Nets, Mattancherry Palace, and the vibrant Vasco da Gama Square.

SPOT.
2

MARINE DRIVE

This picturesque promenade in Kochi offers stunning views of the backwaters and the city skyline. Visitors can enjoy leisurely walks along the waterfront, boat rides on the backwaters, and delicious street food from the numerous stalls.

SPOT.
3

HILL PALACE MUSEUM

Located in Tripunithura, this museum is Kerala's first heritage museum and houses a vast collection of artifacts belonging tothe erstwhile Maharaja of Cochin. The museum also features beautiful gardens and traditional Kerala architecture.

SPOT.
4

BOLGATTY PALACE AND ISLAND RESORT

Situated on Bolgatty Island in Kochi's Vembanad Lake, this heritage resort offers luxurious accommodation with views of the serene backwaters. The island also houses an 18-hole golf course as well as scenic gardens and parks.
