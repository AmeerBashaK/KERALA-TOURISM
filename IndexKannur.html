PAYYAMBALAM BEACH

This scenic beach is known for its golden sands, clear blue waters, and a well-maintained garden area. It's a great place for relaxing, swimming, and enjoying beautiful sunsets.

SPOT.
2

St.ANGELO'S FORT

This historic fort was built by the first Portuguese Viceroy of India and offers stunning views of the Arabian Sea. Visitors can explore the fort's architecture and learn about its intriguing history.

SPOT.
3

ARAKKAL MUSEUM

Housed in the palace of the Arakkal royal family, this museum showcases artifacts that reflect the heritage of this Muslim dynasty. It offers insights into their lifestyle, culture, and traditions.

SPOT.
4

MUZHAPPILANGAD BEACH

Known as Kerala's only drive-in beach, Muzhappilangad offers an exhilarating experience for visitors who can drive along its 4 km-long shoreline while enjoying panoramic views of the sea.
