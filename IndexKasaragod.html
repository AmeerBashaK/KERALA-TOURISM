BEKAL FORT

This 17th-century fort is one of the largest and best-preserved forts in Kerala. Situated on the shores of the Arabian Sea, Bekal Fort offers stunning views of the surrounding landscape and is a popular tourist destination.

2

KAPPIL BEACH

Located near Bekal, Kappil Beach is a serene and picturesque beach offering a tranquil atmosphere to relax amidst nature's beauty. The scenic backwaters at Kappil are perfect for boat rides and birdwatching.

SPOT.
3

Chandragiri Fort

This historical fort situated on the banks of the Chandragiri River offers panoramic views of Kasaragod town and neighboring areas. The fort has significant historical importance and provides visitors with insights into the region's past.

SPOT.
4

Ananthapura Lake Temple

Known as the only lake temple in Kerala, Ananthapura Temple is dedicated to Lord Vishnu and has a unique setting amidst a beautiful lake filled with fish. It's an important pilgrimage site as well as an architectural marvel.
